About MnemAgent

Persistent memory for AI agents - beliefs earn their place through conviction, recall utility, and decay.

  • Nodes are stored memories: facts, preferences, and persona traits.
  • Colors show category — persona, system state, or preference.
  • Synapses connect semantically related beliefs in the cortex.
  • Vividness fades when memories are unused; strong recalls stay bright.
